About Us

We are in the business of assisting travellers with advice and making their visas application process painless. Visawired are specialists based in Johannesburg, South Africa and have over 17 years of experience.

Leading experts in our industry describe Visawired as dedicated, trusted, and qualified professionals always placing the applicants interests first.

Our advantage is derived from vast experience of the complex systems and customer focused attention. The Visawired team fully understands the requirements and just how daunting the processes can be. We are committed to serving you.
